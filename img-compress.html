Why Compress Your Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times (improves SEO ranking)
  • Reduced bandwidth usage and hosting costs
  • Better user experience on mobile devices
  • Easier sharing via email and messaging apps
  • Optimized images for social media platforms

Best Practices for Image Compression

For optimal results:

  • Use WEBP format for the best quality/size ratio (supported by most modern browsers)
  • Aim for 60-80% compression for most web images
  • Resize images to their display dimensions before uploading
  • Test different compression levels to find the sweet spot for your needs
